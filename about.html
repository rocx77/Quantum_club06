About Us

Our journey into the world of Quantum Computing

Our Story

The Quantum Club at C.V. Raman Global University was founded in 2024 with a vision to inspire and engage students in the rapidly growing field of quantum computing. Starting with small workshops and discussions, the club has evolved into a vibrant hub where ideas meet innovation.

We are a student-driven initiative focused on learning, research, and collaboration. Our mission is to equip members with the skills, knowledge, and curiosity needed to thrive in the quantum era.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To nurture quantum talent, promote research, and foster collaboration across disciplines.

Our Vision

To establish CVRGU as a leading hub for innovation and exploration in quantum technologies.

Our Journey
