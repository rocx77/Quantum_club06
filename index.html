Google researchers recently announced a quantum processor that demonstrates error correction improvements, a key step towards scalable quantum computing. This could pave the way for solving problems classical computers struggle with.

Scientists in China have successfully transmitted quantum-encrypted messages across 1,200 km using satellite links, pushing forward the dream of a secure global quantum internet.
